Number Crunch

A strategic number puzzle game - select rectangular groups that sum to the target!

How to Play:

  • Click and drag to select a rectangular group of numbers
  • The selected numbers must sum to the target value
  • Successful matches remove the numbers (no shifting)
  • Use the Scramble button (3 uses per level) to reorganize remaining tiles
  • Scrambling takes 2 seconds and can help when stuck
  • Beat the enemy by reaching higher scores!
  • The enemy attacks every 2 seconds - don't let your health reach zero!

Game Mechanics:

  • Selection: Click and drag for rectangular selection
  • Matching: Sum must equal target number
  • Scoring: Points based on selection size
  • Levels: Harder targets and stronger enemies
  • Time Pressure: Enemy attacks every 2 seconds
